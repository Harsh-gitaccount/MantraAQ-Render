Problem Statement

Agricultural Challenge

Bihar produces 42% of India's Singhara, mostly unprocessed and unbranded. High perishability causes significant farmer losses.

Consumer Need

Consumers lack healthy, affordable gluten-free snacks with 101M+ diabetics in India (ICMR 2023).

Market Opportunity

Gluten-free market growing at 12–14% CAGR, ₹8,500Cr market size.

Our Solution

❄️

Cold Processing

Cold-processed Singhara flour and healthy snacks retaining nutrients and delivering better taste.

📱

QR Traceability

QR-traceable supply chain connects brand directly to farmers and builds consumer trust.

💚

Health Focused

Diabetic-friendly, gluten-free, clean-label products with energy bars and sweeteners as innovations.
